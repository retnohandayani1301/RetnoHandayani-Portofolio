About

"Sharing what actually works — not just what sounds good."

I'm Retno Handayani, an AI-Assisted Digital Creator based in Indonesia. I write and build the things digital brands actually need to sell: scroll-stopping captions, persuasive copy, ghostwritten ebooks, and ready-to-sell digital products.

I run my own digital bookstore, RH Finds — a growing catalog of guides on AI tools, freelancing, and digital marketing, published on Winme.id and Lynk.id. Every skill I offer clients started as something I tested on my own brand first, from cover to copy to launch.

I combine sharp copywriting instincts with a modern AI workflow (Claude, ChatGPT, and design tools) to move fast without cutting corners. I'm certified in Claude 101 and AI Fluency for Small Businesses through Anthropic Academy, and I apply that fluency to everything I write.
